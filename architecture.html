Architecture

The Taj Mahal is a remarkable blend of various architectural styles, including Mugal, Persian and Indian. It is made of white marble, which was brought from different parts of India and Central Asia.

The mausoleum is know for its symmetrical design, itricate carvings and iconic central dome. The surrounding gardens and the Yamuna river add to its beauty, making it a masterpiece of Islamic architecture.
