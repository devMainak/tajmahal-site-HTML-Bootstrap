Overview

The Taj Mahal is one of the most famous monuments in the world. It is located in Agra, India, and is a symbol of eternal love. This stunning white marble mausoleum was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan is memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

The Taj Mahal is not only a UNESCO World Heritage Site but also considered one of the most beautiful architectural masterpieces in history.
